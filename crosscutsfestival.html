BCI Student Council

Designing social media campaigns to be used by the Brantford Collegiate Institute Student Council to increase attendance at their events and engage their students

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Context

Goal: Design posters, social media posts, tickets, geofilters, and apparel for the Brantford Collegiate Student Council

Role: Co-President of the Brantford Collegiate Student Council

Team: My fellow co-president, ten committee directors, five supervising teachers, and fifty general members.

Duration: September 2019 – June 2019 (10 Months)

Skills: User Research, Visual Design, Marketing

Responsibility: Lead a student council of over sixty members to develop engaging marketing materials that will increase school spirit and participation at events.

Outcome

• An increase of attendance by 125% at school dances and events

• Growth of social media followers and engagement by 40%

• Complete redesign of our approach to planning events

• An amazing year that we can look back fondly on!

Defining the Problem

Research Goals:

• Recognize the strengths and weaknesses of our current social media campaigns

• Understand what events and types of activities interest students the most

• Examine what attracts teachers to volunteer their supervision

• Analyze the interaction between students and school events

• Identify solutions utilized by other local Student Councils and past leadership to fix similar issues
