Health Educators Reaching Other Students

HEROS, or Health Educators Reaching Other Students, is a youth advocacy group at the Brant County Health Unit. Whether it be hosting movie nights to advocate for the removal of smoking in youth-rated movies, organizing an art gallery to promote plain and standardized packaging, or hosting discussions about the dangers of e-cigarettes, this group has provided me with an outlet for my passion for the well-being of my community. In addition to utilizing my graphic design abilities to create an effective outreach on social media, I have also individually worked with members on their graphic design and public speaking to ensure that there will be a strong foundation after I leave.

Highlight: Through our group and many others across Canada, we were successful in our Plain and Standardized Packaging campaign; in May 2018, Bill S-5 implemented restrictions on brand colours, logos, and graphics on tobacco packages.
